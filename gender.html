Share of Registered Voters by Confession

(text to change)

This map shows changes in percentage share of registered voters by confession. Arrows indicate and increase or decrease in the percentage of registered voters since 2009. You can mouse over any district to see voter registration rates by confession since 2005

Note: The percentage of the electorate registered to a given confession will decrease even if the total number of voters registered to that confession were to increase if registrations of other confessions increases at a greater rate.
